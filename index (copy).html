About Us

Kushiara International Convention Hall is a premier event venue in Sylhet, Bangladesh, dedicated to providing an exceptional experience for all types of gatherings, from grand weddings to corporate conferences.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, elegant design, and versatile spaces are tailored to meet the unique needs of each event, ensuring a memorable experience for every guest. Committed to excellence in service, we offer comprehensive event solutions, including advanced audio-visual technology, professional catering, and personalized support, making us the preferred choice for both local and international clients.

Services

01

Event Hosting

Kushiara International Convention Hall offers versatile spaces ideal for hosting a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate meetings, seminars, and cultural functions. Each event is customized to suit your specific needs, ensuring a seamless and memorable experience for all attendees.

02

Catering Services

Our in-house catering team provides a variety of menu options, from traditional Bangladeshi cuisine to international dishes. With a focus on quality and taste, we ensure that every meal is prepared to the highest standards, complementing the overall event experience.

03

Event Management

Our dedicated event management team provides comprehensive planning and coordination services to ensure your event runs smoothly from start to finish. From venue setup and decoration to scheduling and logistics, we handle all the details so you can focus on enjoying your special occasion.
